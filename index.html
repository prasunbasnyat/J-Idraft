Our Services

Groceries

We offer a wide variety of fresh produce, dairy products, and household essentials.

Pantry Staples

J & I Variety provides a selection of basic pantry items, including rice, pasta, canned goods, cereals, and condiments. These items are ideal for everyday cooking and meal preparation.

Snacks

A wide variety of snacks are available, such as chips, cookies, candies, and nuts. The store also carries healthier snack options, including granola bars and dried fruits.

Beverages

The beverage section includes soft drinks, juices, bottled water, and energy drinks. You can also find hot beverages like coffee and tea, available both for takeout and as packaged goods.

Household Essentials

In addition to food items, J & I Variety stocks various household essentials, including cleaning supplies, paper products, and personal care items like soap, shampoo, and toothpaste.

Beer and Wine Selection

Beers: The beer selection includes popular domestic brands like Budweiser, Coors, and Miller, as well as imported options such as Heineken and Corona. You might also find craft beers from local breweries, depending on availability. These options range from light lagers to rich stouts and IPAs.

Wines: The wine section features a variety of red, white, and sparkling wines.
